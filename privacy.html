Privacy Policy for Item Search GPT

Last updated: 31 October 2025

This GPT, Item Search, helps users locate and compare collectible postcards listed on public online marketplaces such as Delcampe, eBay, Philasearch, HipPostcard, and Bidspirit.

Information Collected

  • The GPT does not store or share any personal information.
  • Search inputs (keywords, filters, or uploaded postcard images) are processed temporarily to perform searches and are not retained.
  • No cookies, analytics, or third-party tracking tools are used by this GPT.
  • External sites you visit (e.g., marketplace links) have their own privacy policies.

Data Processing

All processing occurs within OpenAI’s systems and the connected search API hosted at postcard-search.vercel.app. No data is stored by the developer or transferred to third parties.

Your Choices

You may stop using this GPT at any time. You can also delete your ChatGPT conversations to remove any stored data from your OpenAI account.
